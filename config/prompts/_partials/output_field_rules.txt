=== STANDARDIZED OUTPUT FIELDS ===

- temporal_info: null OR {{date_mentioned, is_superseded, supersedes_reason}}

- parse_notes: Brief explanation of what was extracted
  - For simple name-only inputs: "Direct bunk request" or "Direct separation request"
  - For names with context: Include relevant context (nickname, session, rationale)
  - For multi-name extractions: Each name gets its own parse_notes

- reasoning: Why you categorized this as this request_type
  - What keywords or patterns led to your interpretation
  - Why you chose this request_type
  - Any ambiguity or uncertainty in the input

- list_position: Position in multi-item extractions (0-indexed)
